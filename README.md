End-to-end MySQL project analyzing 100k+ Brazilian E-commerce orders. Developed complex SQL queries to calculate business KPIs, Month-over-Month (MoM) growth, customer retention, 
and delivery performance to provide actionable business insights.


Objective: Is project ka maqsad Olist (Brazil's largest department store) ke data se business ki health check karna tha. Maine MySQL ka use karke ye pata lagaya ki:

Kaunse States aur Cities sabse zyada revenue generate kar rahe hain.

Delivery efficiency kaisi hai (On-time vs Late deliveries).

Customers ka Payment behavior kya hai (Installments vs Full payment).

Kaunsi Product Categories top-performing hain.

Technical Skills Demonstrated:

Data Cleaning: Handling NULL values and formatting dates.

Complex Aggregations: SUM, AVG, COUNT for Business KPIs.

Advanced SQL: Window Functions (RANK, LAG, LEAD) aur CTEs (Common Table Expressions).

Business Intelligence: Monthly sales growth aur customer retention analysis.
